Extorious formed several years ago with a group of Minecraft friends. Since then, we have all split ways, but now a few of us are bringing it back. After establishing the guild, we have become a friendly, active, and competitive community on the Hypixel network. We play all games (mainly skywars), and are often talking to each other on Discord. We host guild parties every Friday and Saturday, and we typically also have smaller guild parties multiple times a week. In addition, we occasionally host UHC and other guild events on a private server. We currently have the tag [✿EXO✿] in the dark green or yellow color. We have a best of #3 BEST Skywars Guild and #7 BEST Total Wins Guild. Overall, our guild is a fun place to hangout and meet new people, we would love to have you as a part of our team!
